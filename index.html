About Us

Welcome to Sabrina's Cookbook. I'm Sabrina Huynh, a part-time homecook who's inspired by top-world chefs! The main reason why I created this cookbook is to let all of my audiences know that you don't have to be a master chef to get cooking. Why? Because I am here to help you test out all the best recipes you can possibly find. I'll take the failure to let you succeed!

Happy cooking!


Sabrina Huynh

My name is Vinh Nhan and I'm the web designer of Sabrina's Cookbook. Sabrina and I are friends for almost 10 years and we both find cooking to be our passion. We often share lots of recipe as well as cooking experience to each other and thought that it'd be fun to share it with you guys too. I hope you also enjoy this cookbook as much as we do!

Cheers,


Vinh Nhan
